[**`Equator`**](https://equator-website.vercel.app/) is a friendly open-source community, you can learn a lot from here.

:::

Fully On-Chain Game is a wonderful narrative 💭, and the Equator Community was born as a result. We are committed to researching the technology related to fully on-chain game 🎯, helping more people understand it. Meanwhile, we are a developer open-source community, let us together build an unparalleled ecosystem 🌏!

全链游戏是个很美妙的叙事 💭，赤道社区也因此应运而生。我们致力于全链游戏相关的技术的研究 🎯，帮助更多的人了解它。同时我们也是开发者开源社区，让我们共同建立一个无与伦比的生态 🌏！

:::tip Declaration

We are constantly learning and appreciate everyone's contributions! Due to our limited capabilities, if there are any mistakes, we welcome everyone to provide active feedback!

:::

:::tip 申明

我们在不断学习中，感谢各位的贡献！由于我们的能力有限，如果出现错误，欢迎大家积极反馈！

:::
